The Efficiency Seeker (Primary Persona):
Demographics: Typically 25-45 years old, experienced online ESL teachers,
often working independently or for multiple platforms. Tech-savvy and open
to new tools.
Goals: Maximize teaching hours, minimize prep time, achieve better worklife balance, increase income potential by taking on more students.
Pain Points: Exhausted by repetitive lesson planning, struggle to find highquality, adaptable materials quickly, feel overwhelmed by administrative
tasks.
Motivations: Desire for more personal time, professional growth, and
financial stability. Values efficiency and smart solutions.
How PlanwiseESL Helps: Directly addresses time constraints, provides
instant, high-quality lessons, and allows them to focus on teaching.
Key Message Resonance: "Reclaim Your Time. Reignite Your Teaching."
The Engagement Innovator (Secondary Persona):
Demographics: Ages 28-50, passionate about teaching, constantly seeking
new ways to make lessons interactive and fun. May have a background in
education or instructional design.
Goals: Keep students actively engaged, cater to diverse learning styles,
deliver memorable and effective lessons, build strong student relationships.
Pain Points: Students losing interest, difficulty finding varied and interactive
activities, lessons feeling stale or repetitive, time-consuming customization.
Motivations: Driven by student success and a desire to be a highly effective
and inspiring educator. Values creativity and impact.
How PlanwiseESL Helps: Provides instantly generated, adaptable, and
engaging content, enabling tailored lessons for diverse learning styles.
Key Message Resonance: "Transform Your Impact."
The Aspiring Professional (Tertiary Persona):
Demographics: Younger teachers (22-30) or those new to online ESL, seeking
to establish themselves. May be less experienced but eager to learn and
improve.
Goals: Deliver professional and well-structured lessons, build confidence,
gain credibility, establish a strong teaching reputation.
Pain Points: Lack of experience in lesson structuring, uncertainty about best
practices, limited access to resources, fear of underperforming.
Motivations: Desire for professional development, confidence in their
teaching abilities, and a clear path to excellence.
How PlanwiseESL Helps: Ensures consistent teaching excellence with wellstructured, comprehensive, and CEFR-aligned lessons, boosting confidence
regardless of experience.
Key Message Resonance: "Consistent Teaching Excellence
